Prediction of future viewership information for any Television show is essential to designing advertisements, optimising profits from these ads, and also analysing success and efficiency of past promotional campaigns. However, usually, such predictions are based on just previous viewership records and promotions without taking user opinion, such as tweets, in account.
  • Identifying and visualising the relation between general twitter user opinion and TV viewership of television shows.
  • Forecasting, i.e., predicting future values of audience count of these events based on tweets and historical viewership data
